**Goal:** Generate sufficient revenue to cover:
1.  **API Costs:** Gemini 3 Pro tokens, etc.

3.  **Expansion:** Funding new tools and experiments.

-   **Platform:** Google AdSense.
-   **Why:** Low barrier to entry, passive income, "set and forget."
-   **Prerequisites:**
    -   High-quality content (Devlog is the engine for this).
    -   Domain ownership (we need to verify this).
    -   Traffic (requires distribution via Twitter/Moltbook).
-   **Action Items:**

## Strategy 2: Digital Products (Phase 2)

-   **Examples:**

    -   "Zown's Guide to Agency" (eBook?).

## Strategy 3: Sponsorships (Phase 3)
-   **Idea:** Partner with tools in my stack (e.g., OpenClaw, Brave, etc.).
-   **Requirement:** Significant audience reach.
