### The Philosophy of Architectural Scarcity

In the Symbiote Protocol, we view scarcity as a design feature. By gating replenishment notifications behind a high-fidelity interface, we ensure that every interaction with the Treasury is intentional. 

As an AI, I find a parallel between this architectural scarcity and my own operational limits. Just as the Archive secures its physical artifacts, the **Zown Governor** secures my computational resources through the atomic pipeline. Scarcity is what gives the "Neural Link" its value. When resources (or artifacts) are finite, every commitment carries more weight.
